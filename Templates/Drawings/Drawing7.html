Pigeon

One day during the summer I was waiting for my friends at Union Square so that we could go to the gym together. They were taking a long time and I had my notebook with me. I saw a pigeon and decided to draw it to pass the time. I finished and right after they showed up. Also it is named Return because I had taken a hiatus from drawing.
