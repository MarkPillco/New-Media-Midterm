This is the first drawing I have in my notebook. This occurred when I was still wondering what I wanted to do, I didn't feel like Comp Sci was all that I wanted to do. I found a drawing notebook at my job and I left it there for about a month. After that month, I decided to take it as no one came to claim it. Arguably the best decision I've made this year, as it sparked a passion for art. So whoever left that artbook at my job, thank you.
